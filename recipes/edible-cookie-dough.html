Edible Cookie Dough

Description

This edible cookie dough is quick easy and customizable to your cookie dough preferences. It's a tastier alternative to baking home-made cookies and a lot faster to make.
Please keep in mind that this is a COOKIE DOUGH ONLY.

prep time cook time servings days kept
5 minutes 5 minutes 6 up to 10 in fridge

Ingredients

  • 1/2c light Brown Sugar
  • 1/2c vegan butter
  • 3tbsp white sugar
  • 1c heat treated all-purpose flour
  • 1tbsp Non-dairy milK
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract
  • pinch of Salt
  • 1/4-1/2c Dairy Free chocolate chips

Steps to make this meal

  1. Start with heat treating the flour in the microwave for increments of 30 seconds until the flour is 160 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine everything but the milk and chocolate chips. Mix until ingredients form a crumbly consistency.
  3. Add the milk and stir well again.
  4. Fold in the chocolate chips until they stick into the mixture well.
