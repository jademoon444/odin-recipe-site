Edible Cookie Dough

Description

prep time

10 minutes

storage time

up to 10 days in refrigerator

Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar
  • coconut oil
  • Almond flour
  • Non-dairy milK
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Salt
  • Dairy Free chocolate chips

Steps to make this meal

  1. In a medium bowl, combine everything but the milk and chocolate chips. Mix until ingredients form a crumbly consistency.
  2. Add the milk and stir well again.
  3. Fold in the chocolate chips until they stick into the mixture well.
